Kebabche

About:

Kebapche are delicious meat kebabs, made with a variety of meats. No need to worry if you don’t have beef! Make it entirely with pork, or use ground lamb if you can find some. The only thing that’s mandatory here is meat, salt, pepper, and cumin.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 0.33 lb ground beef
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • ¼ cup beer
  • 1 egg
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix the ground pork and ground beef together in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add cumin, salt, pepper, beer. Beat egg in a separate bowl and add to meat.
  3. Thoroughly mix ingredients til they are homogeneous. Let it marinate on counter for 1 hour.
  4. Take your hands and begin rolling meat into sausages, about the length of a hot dog, but wider.
  5. Heat up a skillet on medium heat or fire up the grill. Put the kebapche in.
  6. Cook for about 7 minutes, turning them at least 2 times, to achieve equal browning and to cook the inside.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
